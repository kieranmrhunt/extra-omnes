EXTRA OMNES

"Everyone else, out." · The conclave of 1492 · Twenty-three princes, one throne, five days that bought the keys of Heaven.

Innocent VIII is dead. The Sistine Chapel is sealed. Outside, Rome murders itself by night; inside, France's gold, Naples' threats and Milan's serpent circle the empty throne. Choose your cardinal — one of the twenty-three who were truly there, or add absent electors for an alternate College — and win the tiara, or sell the making of it.
